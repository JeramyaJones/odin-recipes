Ratburger

A ratburger is a sandwich consisting of a cooked meat patty on a bun or roll. Ratburger are traditionally made with ground rat and served with onions, tomatoes, lettuce, ketchup, and other garnishes. This dish used to be made with ground beef before the world ran low on cows.

Ingredents

  • Ground rat
  • Cheese
  • Buns
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoe
  • onion
  • Pickle
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Mayo

Steps

  1. Season the ground rat with salt and pepper.
  2. Form ground rat into pattys.
  3. Grill rat pattys adding cheese when nearly finished.
  4. place pattys on buns and add veggies/sauces as desired.
  5. Enjoy!
