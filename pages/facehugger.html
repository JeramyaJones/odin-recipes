Facehugger

For fans of Ridley Scott’s Alien facing a holiday at home alone this year, there’s no better time to try a recipe that might not be so at home during a traditional family get-together this holiday season. While the roasted chicken is a fairly traditional choice for Christmas and other winter holidays, why not put a xenomorph-style spin on the old classic?

Ingredents

  • 1 whole chicken
  • Desired spice rub & glaze
  • 3 feet of sausage casing
  • Butcher’s twine
  • 1 additional boneless chicken breast
  • 1/4 cup chicken broth
  • 0.5 oz (roughly 1/2 teaspoon) transglutaminase powder
  • 8 crab legs, 4 left and 4 right (precooked)

Steps

  1. Spatchcock the chicken.
  2. Remove the wings and legs, leaving the breasts and thighs intact with the skeleton of the bird.
  3. Flip the bird over, breast side up, and carefully remove the wings
  4. Press the bird flat into the board, placing gentle pressure on breasts with the palm of your hands. There will be some crunches.
  5. To create the tail combine the additional boneless chicken breast and transglutaminase powder in the bowl of a food processor along with whatever spices are being used on the chicken.
  6. Stuff the sausage casing.
  7. Add the tail to the chicken
  8. Place your creation into a 350℉ (175℃) oven for forty minutes.
  9. Add the crab legs
  10. Enjoy!
